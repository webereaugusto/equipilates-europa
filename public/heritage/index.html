Why this congress matters

A Living Tribute to Joseph and Clara Pilates

Pilates Heritage is more than an international congress. It is a ceremonial and educational encounter designed to preserve authentic practice while inspiring the next generation of instructors and studio owners.

The 2026 edition becomes even more symbolic with the recognition of Joseph Pilates Platz in his birthplace, reconnecting the global Pilates community with its historical roots.

The presence of Kathy Corey and Lolita San Miguel - two of the greatest living legends in Pilates - elevates this gathering to a rare moment where legacy, lineage, and excellence come together in one stage.

Method & manufacturing

Joseph Pilates still mentors how we build equipment today

His standards of control, precision, and durability are the reference we return to when engineering every studio line - from spring behavior to frame stability.

Equipilates translates that discipline into contemporary manufacturing: premium equipment produced in Spain for Europe, and in Brazil for the Americas, with the same obsession for quality, consistency, and long-term studio performance.
